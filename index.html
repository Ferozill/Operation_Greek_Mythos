Operation: Greek Mythos

an interactive Makey-Makey game based on Hasbro's Operation

by Brandon Elkins, Caleb Fink, and Ashwin Kumar

Operation: Greek Mythos plays pretty much the same as Operation, the difference being that the game board is hooked up to the computer. When the tweezers touch the sides of the board cavities, the minotaur on the screen will react in anger indicating that the player has failed at getting the piece out safely.

P.S. Press space to anger the minotaur without a gameboard.
